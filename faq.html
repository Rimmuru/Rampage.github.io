Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My game is a cracked version and Rampage
wont work, what can I do?

A: Buy the game, very limited help will be offered
for cracked versions of the game. However there is one version that
is able to work for cracked versions. which can be found
in the links tab.

Q: What are the panel things in the top right corner
and how can I disable them?

A: They are window activators, press "x" to enable your mouse (by default)
and use your mouse to click on them. They give you information
about Rampage and your game. You can disable them by going to
Settings -> Extended Ui -> Disable Window Activators.

Q: How do I disable the open prompt button?

A: Go to Settings -> Extended Ui -> Disable Open Info.
